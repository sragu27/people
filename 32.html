Description

My name is Bharti and I am here to raise funds for my daughter Lavanya who is 1 year old. Lavanya lives with family, in Raipur, Chhattisgarh.

Lavanya is suffering from Bone Marrow Failure Syndromes (IBMFS) since the age of 2 months. She is going for Medication at Private children's Hospital at Raipur, Chhattisgarh . My baby is requiring blood transfusion every month, So far we have done so many consultation in a private hospital at Bengaluru, Karnataka . Currently she is admitted and need Bone Marrow Transplantation.

I am a housewife and my Husband Vijayendra Kumar Sao working in Private Sector It is impossible for us to finance daughter's Treatment in these circumstances. Therefore I am looking to raise a Fund.

Until now, we have spent about Rs.5,00,000/-. we have arranged amount from loans. In next few days we need Rs.35,00,000/- more for Further Treatment. Please come forward to support my cause.
